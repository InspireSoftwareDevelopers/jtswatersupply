About Us

JTS water Supply is a pioneering initiative taken by a team of highly experienced corporate professionals to address the perennial water shortage that many residential layouts in Bengaluru have been experiencing over the years. JTS Water Supply offers a suite of smart solutions to residential layouts to gain easy access to reliable water supply. To facilitate this, JTS water Supply has developed a tech-enabled network of water suppliers. The network systematically captures data on water availability, movement of water tankers, demand for water in different localities and layouts, demand fulfilment and turnaround time.
